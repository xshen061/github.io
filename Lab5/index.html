Vicky LaBel

Registered Physiotherapist

Vicki graduated with her Bachelor of Science in Physiotherapy from University of MacMaster in 2006. For 15 years she has worked in hospitals, in community care and in private clinics, specifying in the field of orthopaedics and sports injuries.

Nick Brown

Registered Physiotherapist

Nick completed his Bachelor of Science degree in Physiotherapy from the University of Toronto in 1989 and is a member of the College of Physiotherapists of Ontario. He is professonal in a wide range of techniques: acupuncture; craniosacral therapy; lymphatic drainage therapy; visceral manipulation; progress by exercise and other treatment forms.
